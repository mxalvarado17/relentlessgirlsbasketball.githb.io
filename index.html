Our Home Court

Head Coach Donna Huhn has coached both womens and mens basketball for over 20 year. Coach Donna is currently head coach of Relentless Girsl basketball and Carlsabd High school. Our teams practice at Eden Park on San Elijo Rd.

Team Spirit

Relentless is about growing up with a team of supportive athletes that push you to be your best. We cherish the memories and relationships made on and off the court. Not only do we play in local tournaments, we travel all over the country, playing the sport we love.
